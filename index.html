Ancient Pantheons

Explore the Divine Mythologies of Ancient Civilizations

Greek Pantheon

Zeus

Zeus

God of: Sky, Thunder, Lightning, King of Gods

Spouse: Hera

Parents: Cronus and Rhea

Children: Athena, Apollo, Artemis, Hermes, Dionysus, Ares, and many more

Symbol: Thunderbolt, Eagle, Oak

Hera

Hera

Goddess of: Marriage, Women, Family, Queen of Gods

Spouse: Zeus

Parents: Cronus and Rhea

Children: Ares, Hebe, Hephaestus, Eileithyia

Symbol: Peacock, Cow, Pomegranate

Poseidon

Poseidon

God of: Sea, Earthquakes, Horses

Spouse: Amphitrite

Parents: Cronus and Rhea

Children: Triton, Theseus, Polyphemus, Orion

Symbol: Trident, Horse, Dolphin

Aphrodite

Aphrodite

Goddess of: Love, Beauty, Pleasure, Passion

Spouse: Hephaestus (affair with Ares)

Parents: Born from sea foam (or Zeus & Dione)

Children: Eros, Harmonia, Aeneas

Symbol: Dove, Rose, Swan, Myrtle

Apollo

Apollo

God of: Sun, Music, Poetry, Prophecy, Healing

Spouse: None (many lovers)

Parents: Zeus and Leto

Twin: Artemis

Symbol: Lyre, Bow, Laurel, Sun

Artemis

Artemis

Goddess of: Hunt, Wilderness, Moon, Chastity

Spouse: None (virgin goddess)

Parents: Zeus and Leto

Twin: Apollo

Symbol: Bow, Deer, Moon, Cypress

Ares

Ares

God of: War, Courage, Bloodshed

Lover: Aphrodite

Parents: Zeus and Hera

Children: Phobos, Deimos, Harmonia, Eros

Symbol: Spear, Helmet, Dog, Vulture

Athena

Athena

Goddess of: Wisdom, War Strategy, Crafts

Spouse: None (virgin goddess)

Parents: Zeus (born from his head)

Symbol: Owl, Olive Tree, Shield, Spear

Roman Pantheon

Jupiter

Jupiter

God of: Sky, Thunder, King of Gods

Spouse: Juno

Greek Equivalent: Zeus

Children: Mars, Vulcan, Minerva, Apollo

Symbol: Eagle, Thunderbolt, Oak

Juno

Juno

Goddess of: Marriage, Women, Queen of Gods

Spouse: Jupiter

Greek Equivalent: Hera

Children: Mars, Vulcan, Juventas

Symbol: Peacock, Cow, Lily

Neptune

Neptune

God of: Sea, Earthquakes, Horses

Spouse: Salacia

Greek Equivalent: Poseidon

Children: Triton, Arion

Symbol: Trident, Horse, Dolphin

Venus

Venus

Goddess of: Love, Beauty, Desire, Victory

Spouse: Vulcan (affair with Mars)

Greek Equivalent: Aphrodite

Children: Cupid, Aeneas

Symbol: Dove, Rose, Swan, Myrtle

Mars

Mars

God of: War, Agriculture, Guardian of Rome

Lover: Venus

Greek Equivalent: Ares

Parents: Jupiter and Juno

Symbol: Spear, Shield, Wolf, Woodpecker

Minerva

Minerva

Goddess of: Wisdom, Strategic War,Crafts

Spouse: None (virgin goddess)

Greek Equivalent: Athena

Parents: Jupiter

Symbol: Owl, Olive Tree, Aegis

Apollo

Apollo

God of: Sun, Music, Poetry, Prophecy, Healing

Twin Sister: Diana

Greek Equivalent: Apollo

Parents: Jupiter and Latona

Symbol: Lyre, Bow, Laurel, Sun Chariot

Diana

Diana

Goddess of: Hunt, Moon, Wilderness, Childbirth

Spouse: None (virgin goddess)

Greek Equivalent: Artemis

Parents: Jupiter and Latona

Symbol: Bow, Deer, Moon, Torch

Egyptian Pantheon

Ra

Ra

God of: Sun, Creation, King of Gods

Spouse: Hathor/Sekhmet

Children: Shu, Tefnut, Sekhmet, Bastet

Role: Supreme deity, travels across sky daily

Symbol: Sun disk, Falcon, Ankh

Osiris

Osiris

God of: Afterlife, Death, Resurrection, Agriculture

Spouse: Isis

Parents: Geb and Nut

Children: Horus

Symbol: Crook, Flail, Atef Crown

Isis

Isis

Goddess of: Magic, Motherhood, Healing, Wisdom

Spouse: Osiris

Parents: Geb and Nut

Children: Horus

Symbol: Throne, Tyet knot, Wings

Horus

Horus

God of: Sky, Kingship, Protection

Spouse: Hathor

Parents: Osiris and Isis

Children: Four Sons of Horus

Symbol: Falcon, Eye of Horus, Was Scepter

Anubis

Anubis

God of: Mummification, Death, Embalming

Spouse: Anput

Parents: Osiris and Nephthys (or Ra and Nephthys)

Role: Guide of souls to afterlife

Symbol: Jackal, Was Scepter

Sekhmet

Sekhmet

Goddess of: War, Plague, Healing, Vengeance

Spouse: Ptah

Parents: Ra (or created by Ra)

Children: Nefertem

Symbol: Lioness, Solar disk, Uraeus

Set

Set

God of: Chaos, Desert, Storms, Disorder

Spouse: Nephthys

Parents: Geb and Nut

Role: Murdered Osiris, fought with Horus

Symbol: Was Scepter, Set Animal

Hathor

Hathor

Goddess of: Love, Beauty, Music, Motherhood, Joy

Spouse: Horus (or Ra)

Children: Ihy, Horus

Role: Protector of women, patron of musicians

Symbol: Cow, Sistrum, Mirror, Menat

Vedic Pantheon

Indra

Indra

God of: Thunder, Rain, War, King of Gods

Spouse: Indrani (Shachi)

Parents: Dyaus and Prithvi

Children: Jayanta, Jayanti

Symbol: Vajra (thunderbolt), Elephant Airavata

Agni

Agni

God of: Fire, Sacrifices, Divine Messenger

Spouse: Svaha

Parents: Dyaus and Prithvi (or self-created)

Role: Mediator between humans and gods

Symbol: Fire, Ram, Seven tongues of flame

Varuna

Varuna

God of: Water, Oceans, Cosmic Order, Sky

Spouse: Varuni

Role: Guardian of cosmic law (Rita)

Children: Vasistha, Agastya

Symbol: Noose, Makara (sea creature)

Surya

Surya

God of: Sun, Light, Life, Health

Spouses: Saranyu, Chhaya

Parents: Aditi and Kashyapa

Children: Yama, Yamuna, Ashvins, Shani

Symbol: Sun chariot, Seven horses, Lotus

Soma

Soma

God of: Moon, Divine Drink, Plants

Spouses: 27 daughters of Daksha (Nakshatras)

Parents: Atri and Anusuya

Role: Grants immortality through sacred drink

Symbol: Moon, Crescent, Antelope

Rudra

Rudra

God of: Storms, Hunt, Death, Healing

Spouse: Prishni/Rudrani

Role: Wild, destructive aspect (later Shiva)

Children: Maruts (storm gods)

Symbol: Bow and arrows, Trident, Bull

Ushas

Ushas

Goddess of: Dawn, New Beginnings

Parents: Dyaus and Prithvi

Role: Brings light, awakens all living beings

Sibling: Sister of Ratri (Night)

Symbol: Dawn light, Chariot, Red garments

Vayu

Vayu

God of: Wind, Breath, Life Force

Spouse: Bharati

Role: Carries prayers to gods

Children: Hanuman, Bhima

Symbol: Wind, White horse, Deer
